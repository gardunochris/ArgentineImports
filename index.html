    Why Import From Argentina?

    • Help Local Economies
    • Boycot China
    • Faster Turnaround Times

    Argentina has a large abundance in natural resources that allowed them to produce goods and services at a much lower cost compard to other countries.   Argentina has extremely efficient distribution networks and facilities that have made it a top alternative exporting option.

    In response to the world shifting viral spread of "The China Virus" COVID-19, many US based companies are boycotting China by ceasing all imports from them and moving their importing to Argentina.

    Because Argentina is much closer in proximity to the United States than China is, turnaround times can be expected to be much faster in all areas and all goods and services.
